Features

Everything your inbox needs.
Nothing the cloud requires.

Six AI-powered tools that learn your email patterns and work for you — all running locally on your machine.

Smart Labels

AI scans your inbox and suggests contextual labels — Urgent, Client, Newsletter, Invoice, and more. Review suggestions and apply with one click.

Draft Generator

Automatically generates reply drafts for emails that need a response. Supports Reply All with CC preservation. Edit, approve, or discard — you stay in control.

Follow-up Detection

Identifies emails that are waiting for a reply or action. Never miss a follow-up again. AI even drafts a suggested nudge for you.

Contact Extraction

Scans email signatures using NLP to extract names, phone numbers, job titles, and companies. Build a contacts database from years of emails in minutes.

Bulk Mailer

Send personalized emails at scale. Import contacts from CSV or Excel, compose your template, and send batched emails directly through your own email account.

Training Hub

Teach the AI your preferences. Train label categories, reply style, and follow-up priorities. The more you train, the smarter it gets — all stored locally.

How It Works

Set up in minutes. Train over time.

NeuroMail connects to your email account, runs AI locally, and keeps everything on your device.

Install & Sign In

Download the Windows installer and sign in with your Gmail or Outlook account. Your credentials stay on your machine.

Scan Your Inbox

Run your first scan. NeuroMail analyzes your emails locally using a built-in AI model — no data leaves your computer.

Review & Approve

Browse AI-generated labels, drafts, and follow-ups in a clean dashboard. Approve, edit, or dismiss each suggestion.

Train & Improve

Use the Training Hub to teach the AI your email habits. Over time, suggestions become increasingly personalized and accurate.

Privacy by Architecture

NeuroMail processes everything on your device using a local AI model. Your emails, contacts, and training data never leave your computer. No cloud APIs, no third-party AI services, no telemetry. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
